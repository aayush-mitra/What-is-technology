Energy

Aayush Mitra

Energy is the ability or capacity to do work.

Pros

  • Without Energy, technology wouldn't work.
  • There is an abundant amount of types of energy that do not damage the planet, such as wind and solar energy.

Cons

  • Energy cannot be created infinitely. It can only be transfered. If you overuse energy, it will cost a lot.
  • Certain types of energy negatively effect our environment. For example fossil fuels extract chemical energy when burned. Fossil fuels such as coal, oil, and natural gas hurt our planet.
